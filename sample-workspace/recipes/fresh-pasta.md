# Fresh pasta

Serves 4. About 30 minutes, most of it hands-off.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 1 tsp salt
- 3 eggs
- A splash of olive oil

## Method

1. Combine the dry ingredients.
2. Make a well, add the eggs, and bring together.
3. Rest for 20 minutes under a bowl.
4. Roll thin and cut.

> Tip: the dough keeps overnight in the fridge.
